- Evolution

Entrance Page (components):
- Your Name (1-20 char., Allowed: letters, numbers, spaces, -, _, '. No reserved words)
- Pet Name (Same guidelines as Player Name)
- Pet Type (Dog, Cat, Dragon)
- Difficulty (Easy, Normal, Hard) --> (different dollar amounts/difficulty levels)
- Start Game button
- Load Game button for saved states
- Help Page access button
- Pet icon, evolution, and name displayed

Start Page (components): 
- Introduction to who we are as a company (Pet Life)
- Features and purpose of Pet Life
- Who Pet Life is for (teaching youths what it means to manage a business by starting with their pets)

Game Page (components):
- Shop:
    -Food tab(must buy food before feeding. Buy again every week):
        -Basic food($5, Standard meal. 1 energy per dollar.)
        -Premium Food($10, 2 energy per dollar)
        -Deluxe Food($15, 3 energy per dollar)
        -Gourmet Food($25, 4 energy per dollar)
    Toys(better toys reduce daily play requirement, renews weekly):
        -Basic Toy($5, reduces to 4 plays/day required)
        -Standard Toy($10, requires 3 plays/day)
        -Premium Toy($15, 2 plays/day)
        -Deluxe Toy($25, 1 play/day)
    Addons:
        -To-Do schedule. Unlockes schedule feature, costs 2 hours per use. Allows user to track the amount of times the user needs to feed, clean, read book, exercise, and vet visits per day. User must make a new schedule eeryday, but the purchase is one time.
