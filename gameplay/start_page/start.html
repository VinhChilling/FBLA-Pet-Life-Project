Pet Life

A virtual pet business-life simulator made to help younger students practice responsibility, budgeting, time management, and care.

Begin Help

Why It Exists

Pet Life turns everyday choices into a playable model of care: feed the pet, buy supplies, manage money, rest, exercise, and learn what happens when responsibilities compete for the same day.

Who Built It

The program was created by Deeptanava Roy and Luca Trinh for the 2026 FBLA Intro to Programming event.

Who It Helps

It is designed for young learners who are beginning to understand business, budgeting, routines, and the habit of balancing personal needs with the needs of others.
