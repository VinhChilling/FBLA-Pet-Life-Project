🐶 Help & Information

Purpose/Goal

Welcome to Pet Life! Your mission is to care for your pet while managing your own health, time, and resources. Balance your pet's needs with your personal well-being across multiple days to earn the highest score possible.

Scoring: Each day starts with 100 potential points. Your daily score is determined by pet care, self-care, efficiency, and penalties.

Start & Save

Enter your player name and pet name, choose a pet type, then start the game. Difficulty changes your starting coins and stats.

  • Easy – more starting coins and higher stats
  • Normal – balanced starting resources
  • Hard – fewer coins and tougher conditions

The game saves automatically to local storage, and you can also load or clear your save from the game screen.

Daily Cycle

You have 24 hours per day to complete actions. When your time runs out or you choose to sleep, the day ends and the next day begins.

After sleeping, the new day starts with a visible notification if a random event occurs.

Pet Care Actions

🍖 Feed Pet

Feeding increases your pet's energy and health. You need at least 3 feedings per day to avoid penalties.

⚠️ Underfeeding causes -35 energy, -20 health, and Hungry mood.

🎾 Play with Pet

Playing uses 1 hour and improves mood and health. Aim for 4 plays per day.

⚠️ Insufficient play causes -20 energy, -15 health, and Bored mood.

🛁 Clean Pet

Clean your pet once per day. It costs $3 and 2 hours and improves health by 5.

⚠️ If not cleaned, pet loses energy and health and becomes Dirty.

🏥 Visit Vet

The vet should be visited once every 7 days. It costs $40 and 4 hours and restores health.

⚠️ Missing weekly vet care results in major energy and health penalties.

Player Self-Care

🏃 Exercise

Costs 2 hours, gives +10 health and +10 mood.

📚 Read

Costs 1 hour, gives +3 health and +5 mood.

👯 Hangout

Costs 3 hours, gives +12 health and +20 mood.

💼 Work

Costs 3 hours and earns +$15.

🧹 Do Chores

Costs 2 hours and earns +$8.

Food Choices

The shop offers different food and toy tiers. Better quality food and toys reduce requirements and help your pet recover faster.

Premium food and toys expire after 7 days, after which they revert to basic quality.

⚠️ Keep track of expiration so you don't lose your upgrades.

Fast Food & Healthy Eating

You can choose quick food or healthy meals:

  • Fast food takes 0.5 hours, increases mood, but deducts health.
  • Healthy food takes 0.5 hours, increases health, but lowers mood a little.

To-Do List & Scheduling

Buy the schedule feature in the shop to create a daily to-do list and schedule one action per day.

  • Create To-Do List: costs 2 hours and improves mood.
  • Schedule Action: costs 2 hours and gives a health/mood boost.
  • You can only create a to-do list once per day and schedule once per day.

Random Events

At the beginning of each new day after sleeping, the game may trigger a random event. These events are shown as a notification banner.

  • Sickness – your pet loses health and requires vet care.
  • Extra Hunger – your pet needs more feedings and loses energy.
  • Extra Energy Need – your pet wants more play and gains energy.

Sleep Mechanics

Sleep resets your daily time to 24 hours and gives your pet rest energy. If you sleep with very little time left, both you and your pet take penalties.

⚠️ Sleeping with 7 or fewer hours left triggers sleep punishment.

Game Interface

Use the shop button for upgrades, the sleep button to end your day, and the save indicator to confirm progress is saved. The analytics dashboard shows your performance over time.

End Conditions

Your game ends if your pet's health reaches zero. Keep your pet fed, happy, and healthy to survive as many days as possible.

Scoring System

Daily score is calculated from:

  • Pet care performance
  • Player care performance
  • Time and money efficiency
  • Penalties for neglect or low health/mood

Pet Evolution System

Your pet evolves as you survive more days and keep their health strong. Each stage changes your pet's appearance with visual effects — nurture them well to reach the legendary form!

Evolution Stages

Tips for Evolution

  • Keep pet health above stage thresholds to unlock higher forms.
  • Feed, play, and clean daily — neglected pets evolve slower.
  • Reach day 25+ with 75+ health to unlock the Legendary stage.
  • Your evolution journey appears on the Analytics dashboard after each run.

📈 Understanding the Metrics

Performance Grades

  • A (85-89) - Excellent performance
  • B+ (80-84) - Good performance
  • B (75-79) - Above average
  • C+ (70-74) - Average performance
  • C (65-69) - Below average
  • D (60-64) - Poor performance
  • F (<60) - Failing performance

Efficiency Rate

Measures how effectively you use your available time for productive activities. Higher rates indicate better time management and task completion.

Consistency Index

Tracks how regularly you perform essential activities. Consistency is key to long-term success in Pet Life.

Improvement Rate

Shows your performance trend over time. Helps identify whether you're getting better or need to adjust your strategy.
