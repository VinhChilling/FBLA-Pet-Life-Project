🐶 Help & Information

Purpose/Goal

Welcome to Pet Life! Your mission is to care for your pet while managing your own health, time, and resources. Balance your pet's needs with your personal well-being across multiple days to earn the highest score possible.

Scoring: You start each day with 100 potential points. Your daily score is reduced based on how well you care for your pet and yourself. The better you balance both, the more points you keep!

Rules

🍖 Feeding

Feed your pet at least 3 times per day. Each feeding costs $5 and increases energy by 12.

⚠️ If unfed: -35 energy, -20 health, pet becomes Hungry

🎾 Playing

Play with your pet at least 4 times per day. Each play session costs 1 hour.

⚠️ If not played enough: -20 energy, -15 health, pet becomes Bored

Cleaning

Clean your pet once per day. Costs $3 and 2 hours. Improves health by 5.

⚠️ If not cleaned: -10 energy, -15 health, pet becomes Dirty

Vet Visits

Visit the vet once per week (every 7 days). Costs $30 and 4 hours. Fully restores health to 100.

⚠️ If missed: -50 energy, -50 health, pet becomes Sick

Sleep

Sleep when you're done with daily activities. Average 8+ hours for best results.

⚠️ Less than 6h average: -25 health, -25 mood, -20 points

✓ 8+ hours: +15 health, +15 mood

Self-Care

Take care of yourself daily:

  • Exercise (2h): +10 health, +10 mood
  • Hangout (3h): +12 health, +20 mood
  • Read (1h): +3 health, +5 mood

⚠️ Skip these to lose points and stats!

Currency

Coins are your primary currency. You start with 10 coins and can earn more by doing chores.

Do Chore -2 hours +$8
Feed Pet -$5 +12 energy
Clean Pet -$3, -2h +5 health
Vet Visit -$30, -4h 100 health

💡 Tip: Budget wisely! Vet visits are expensive but essential once per week. Do chores regularly to maintain a healthy coin balance.

Time Management

You have 24 hours each day. When time runs out, you automatically sleep. Plan your activities carefully to fit everything in!

Scoring System

You start each day with 100 potential points. Points are deducted for:

  • Poor sleep habits (6-8h: -10 pts, <6h: -20 pts)
  • Pet health below 80 (-15 pts) or below 50 (-25 pts)
  • Pet energy below 80 (-5 pts) or below 50 (-15 pts)
  • Pet in negative mood state (-10 pts)
  • Not exercising (-10 pts)
  • Not hanging out with friends (-15 pts)
  • Not reading (-5 pts)

Pet Evolution System

Your pet evolves as you survive more days and keep their health strong. Each stage changes your pet's appearance with visual effects — nurture them well to reach the legendary form!
